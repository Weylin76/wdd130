Overview

Purpose

The African Cichlid fish tank website aims to assist novice aquarists in establishing their aquariums successfully. It encompasses all essential topics for setting up a thriving fish tank.

Audience

The target audience for my website includes individuals seeking fundamental knowledge for establishing a new African Cichlid fish tank. The site provides all necessary information conveniently accessible at their fingertips.
